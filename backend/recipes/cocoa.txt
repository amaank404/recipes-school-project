# Description
A rich and decadent hot chocolate made with high-quality cocoa powder, perfect for warming up on a chilly day. This recipe is easy to make and requires minimal ingredients.
~DESCRIPTION_SEP_IDENTIFIER~

# Ingredients
- 2 tablespoons unsweetened cocoa powder
- 1 cup milk (dairy or non-dairy, such as almond or soy milk)
- 1 tablespoon sugar (or to taste)
- 1/4 teaspoon salt
- 1/4 teaspoon vanilla extract (optional)

... 
~INGREDIENTS_SEP_IDENTIFIER~

# Instructions
- In a small saucepan, warm the milk over low heat until it starts to simmer.
- In a separate bowl, whisk together the cocoa powder, sugar, and salt until well combined.
- Gradually whisk the dry mixture into the warmed milk, stirring constantly to avoid lumps.
- Bring the mixture to a gentle simmer, whisking constantly, until the hot chocolate is smooth and hot.
- Remove from heat and stir in the vanilla extract, if using.
- Pour into a large mug and serve immediately.

# Tips and Variations
- Use high-quality cocoa powder for the best flavor.
- Adjust the amount of sugar to your taste.
- Add a shot of espresso for a mocha twist.
- Top with whipped cream, marshmallows, or chocolate shavings for extra indulgence.
- Experiment with different types of milk, such as coconut milk or oat milk, for a creamier or dairy-free version.
- Make a batch of cocoa mix by combining the dry ingredients in a jar and storing it in the pantry for up to 6 months.
- Serve hot cocoa in mason jars or thermoses for a fun and portable treat.
- Try adding a pinch of cinnamon or nutmeg for an extra depth of flavor.
