# Description
A burrito is a traditional Mexican dish consisting of a large flour tortilla wrapped around various fillings such as beans, rice, meats, cheese, and vegetables. It's a versatile meal that can be customized to suit individual tastes and dietary needs.

~DESCRIPTION_SEP_IDENTIFIER~

# Ingredients
- 1 cup cooked white or brown rice
- 1 can black beans, drained and rinsed
- 1 pound ground beef or ground turkey
- 1 medium onion, diced
- 2 cloves garlic, minced
- 1 red bell pepper, diced
- 1 tablespoon chili powder
- 1 teaspoon cumin
- Salt and pepper, to taste
- 8 large flour tortillas
- Shredded cheddar cheese, for serving
- Optional toppings: diced tomatoes, shredded lettuce, sour cream, salsa, avocado, cilantro

... 
~INGREDIENTS_SEP_IDENTIFIER~

# Instructions
- Heat a large skillet over medium-high heat.
- Add the ground beef or turkey and cook until browned, breaking it up into small pieces as it cooks.
- Add the diced onion, garlic, and red bell pepper to the skillet and cook until the vegetables are tender.
- Stir in the chili powder and cumin and cook for 1 minute.
- Add the cooked rice and black beans to the skillet and stir to combine with the meat mixture.
- Warm the tortillas by wrapping them in a damp paper towel and microwaving for 20-30 seconds.
- Spoon about 1/2 cup of the filling onto the center of each tortilla, leaving a small border around the edges.
- Sprinkle shredded cheese on top of the filling.
- Fold the bottom edge of the tortilla up over the filling, then fold in the sides and roll the burrito up tightly.
- Repeat with the remaining ingredients.
- Serve immediately, with optional toppings on the side.

# Tips and Variations
- To make vegetarian or vegan burritos, substitute the ground beef or turkey with roasted vegetables or beans.
- Experiment with different types of cheese, such as queso fresco or feta.
- Add some heat to your burritos with diced jalapenos or serrano peppers.
- Try using different types of tortillas, such as whole wheat or corn tortillas.
